Thank you for trusting Rezuu with your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

This Privacy Policy describes how Rezuu ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information.

1. Introduction

Rezuu is a platform that helps users showcase their professional experiences through video portfolios. By using our services, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Rezuu, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, profile picture, resume, certificates, and videos you upload.
  • Account Credentials: Username and password.
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, links clicked, time spent on pages.
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, IP address.

We collect this information directly from you and through third-party services used by Rezuu.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our service and store certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some parts of our service may not function properly.

Common uses for cookies include session management, remembering user preferences, and analyzing user behavior to improve our services.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Rezuu account
  • Allow you to build and display your profile
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve, personalize, and develop our services
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, or policies

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

4.1 Information Collected from External Viewers of Shared Premium Profiles

Rezuu Premium users have the option to share their professional profiles publicly with external parties, such as employers, recruiters, or collaborators. When an external visitor (someone who does not have a Rezuu account) accesses a shared Premium profile, we may collect limited information to ensure platform integrity and security. This may include:

  • IP address and general location (e.g., city or region)
  • Device and browser type
  • Pages visited within the shared profile
  • Time spent viewing the profile
  • Clicks or interactions within the profile page

This information is collected solely for the purposes of:

  • Ensuring the security and functionality of shared profiles
  • Providing analytics and insights to Premium users about the reach of their shared profiles
  • Improving the experience for both profile owners and their viewers

Important:

We do not use this data to personally identify external viewers, and we do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

By visiting a Rezuu Premium shared profile, you acknowledge and accept this limited data collection for the purposes described above.
